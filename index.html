Inside The Application

Modern Auction Administration

DK's Badminton Club Official Auction App solves the traditional issues of manual auctions by replacing pen-and-paper budgeting with an interactive, instant digital system.

  • Live Auction Dashboard: Displays current bidding player details, photo, base points, active bidders, and live status updates.
  • Firebase Authentication & Secure Login: Restricts operational modifications to verified auctioneers and team managers only.
  • Player & Team Management: Assigns players to squads automatically and deducts bid amounts from the team's total budget.
